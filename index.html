Why EM exists

EM started after I lost someone very close to me. After she was gone, one of the first things I did was go back through our WhatsApp chats just to hear her again. The way she said things. The random voice notes. The inside jokes. All of it still there, just... sitting in my phone.

That's when I realised how much lives in our messages. Not just grief, but everything — the early days of a relationship, the chats with family far away, the friends offering words of encouragement.

EM resurfaces those conversations the way Google Photos resurfaces your pictures. On this day, a year ago. Three years ago. Five. Except instead of photos, it's the actual words. The voice notes. The moment someone said something that brightened your day.

It's still being built (by me, obsessively, with a lot of coffee) and I want the people who get it to be the first to use it.

If this feels like something you've needed, watch this space.... It's coming soon.
